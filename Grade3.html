Grade 3 - Smart Devices Around Us 📱💻

Let’s discover the cool tech tools we see every day and what they do!

🧒 What are Smart Devices?

They are machines that help us talk, learn, play and draw! Like:

  • 📱 Phones - to call and play games
  • 💻 Computers - to learn and draw
  • 🖥️ Tablets - to watch and read

🖐️ Can You Touch It?

Yes! These are called Hardware — the parts we can touch!
