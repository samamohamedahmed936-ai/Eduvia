Let’s stay safe while using the internet!

🔒 What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity means protecting computers and data from bad people (hackers) and harmful programs.

⚠️ Online Threats

  • Viruses 🦠
  • Phishing (fake emails and websites)
  • Hackers breaking into accounts

🛡️ How to Stay Safe?

  • Use strong passwords 🔑
  • Don’t click unknown links 🚫
  • Keep your software updated
  • Use antivirus programs
