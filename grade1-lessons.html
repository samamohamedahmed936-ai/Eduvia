Lesson 1: What is a Computer? 💻

A computer is a smart machine that helps us do many things! We can play games, draw pictures, write stories, and even talk to our friends. It has a screen to see things, a keyboard to type, and a mouse to click. It's like a smart helper at school and home.

Lesson 2: Devices We Use 📱🖥️

We use devices like tablets, phones, and computers every day. Tablets help us read and play games. Phones let us talk and take pictures. Computers help us learn new things. These devices are called “smart” because they can do many things!
